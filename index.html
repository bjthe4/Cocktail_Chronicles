Feeling thirsty? We've curated some easy-to-make home cocktail recipes just for you! Ready to escape the summer heat?

Vodka Cocktails

Moscow Mule

Ingredients:

Vodka, ginger beer, lime juice

Instructions:

Fill a copper mug with ice, add vodka and lime juice, and top with ginger beer. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Vodka Lemonade

Ingredients:

Vodka, lemonade, mint leaves

Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice, add vodka and lemonade, and stir. Garnish with mint leaves

Cosmopolitan

Ingredients:

Vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, lime juice

Instructions:

Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a chilled martini glass, and garnish with a lime twist.

Gin Cocktails

Gin and Tonic

Ingredients:

Gin, tonic water, lime wedge

Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice, add gin, top with tonic water, and garnish with a lime wedge.

Tom Collins

Ingredients:

Gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, soda water

Instructions:

Shake gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice. Strain into a glass filled with ice and top with soda water. Garnish with a lemon slice.

Gin Fizz

Ingredients:

Gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, soda water

Instructions:

Shake gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice. Strain into a glass and top with soda water. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Tequila Cocktails

Margarita

Ingredients:

Tequila, triple sec, lime juice, simple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a salt-rimmed glass filled with ice, and garnish with a lime wheel.

Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients:

Tequila, orange juice, grenadine

Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice, add tequila and orange juice, pour grenadine slowly to create a gradient effect, and garnish with an orange slice and cherry.

Paloma

Ingredients:

Tequila, grapefruit soda, lime juice

Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice, add tequila and lime juice, top with grapefruit soda, and garnish with a lime wedge.

Rum Cocktails

Mojito

Ingredients:

White rum, mint leaves, lime juice, simple syrup, soda water

Instructions:

Muddle mint leaves with lime juice and simple syrup. Add rum and ice, top with soda water, and garnish with a mint sprig.

Piña Colada

Ingredients:

White rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice

Instructions:

Blend all ingredients with ice until smooth, pour into a glass, and garnish with a pineapple slice and cherry.

Daiquiri

Ingredients:

White rum, lime juice, simple syrup

Instructions:

Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a chilled glass, and garnish with a lime wheel.
